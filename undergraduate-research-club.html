Undergraduate Research Club

Students have the opportunity to collaborate on impactful, team-based research projects through the Undergraduate Research Club. This initiative fosters interdisciplinary learning, allowing students to gain hands-on experience, contribute to real-world studies, and develop valuable research skills while working alongside faculty and peers.
