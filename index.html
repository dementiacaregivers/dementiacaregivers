Dementia Research Project

Developing a voice assistance app for informal caregivers.

About the Project

Join our research team in developing an innovative voice assistance app to support caregivers assisting individuals with dementia.

Project Description +

The Dementia Research Project at Kennesaw State University is dedicated to developing AI-powered mobile applications that support caregivers of individuals with dementia. Guided by Professors Modupe Akintomide and Xinye Zhang, our research team is focused on understanding caregivers' challenges and designing technology-driven solutions to ease their burden.

Our approach follows Community-Based Participatory Research (CBPR), where caregivers and individuals with dementia play an active role in shaping our work. Their firsthand experiences provide invaluable insights that guide us in creating meaningful and effective tools.

Currently, we are developing two AI-driven mobile applications:

  • Smart Search Engine – A specialized search tool designed to provide accurate and relevant information tailored to caregivers' needs, acting as a Google-like assistant specifically for dementia care.
  • Eligibility Tool – A resource to help caregivers determine their eligibility for government grants, financial aid, and charity funding, ensuring they receive the necessary support.

To refine these tools, we are conducting research studies, inviting caregivers to participate in surveys and interviews that will shape the final design and functionality of the applications.

Project Department +

This research project is a collaboration between:

  • Department of Computer Science – Developing AI-based solutions for caregiver support.
  • Department of Nursing – Providing expertise on dementia care and caregiver challenges.

Our interdisciplinary approach ensures that both technological innovation and healthcare insights are integrated into our research.

Requirements +

We seek passionate individuals to support our AI-driven dementia research. Key responsibilities include:

  • Data Collection & Analysis – Gathering and interpreting survey and interview data.
  • Literature Review – Researching dementia care and AI applications.
  • AI & Software Support – Assisting in refining AI models and app testing.
  • Community Engagement – Conducting caregiver interviews and gathering insights.
  • Team Collaboration – Contributing ideas and participating in research discussions.

Join us to gain hands-on experience in innovative, interdisciplinary research that directly impacts caregivers.

Mission +

Our mission is to empower dementia caregivers through technology, ensuring they have access to reliable information, financial resources, and personalized support. By leveraging artificial intelligence, we aim to create adaptable tools that cater to the unique needs of each caregiver.

Through this initiative, we strive to:

  • Reduce the emotional and mental strain on caregivers.
  • Provide easily accessible and accurate dementia care information.
  • Support caregivers in securing financial aid and resources.
  • Foster a research-driven, community-oriented approach to improving dementia caregiving.

By actively involving caregivers in our research, we ensure that our solutions are practical, relevant, and impactful for those who need them the most.
